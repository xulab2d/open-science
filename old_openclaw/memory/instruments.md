# Instruments & Systems

## Cryostats / Measurement Platforms

### Attodry 911
- **Users**: Zengde, Weijie
- **Sample**: WJL_C7 (tMoTe₂)
- **Capabilities**: PL, low-temperature optical

### Old Attodry
- **Users**: Zengde, Weijie
- **Sample**: A5 AAtA dot (tMoTe₂ quantum dot)
- **Capabilities**: PL, spectroscopy

### OptiCool
- **Users**: Yue, others (CrSBr-BFA experiments)
- **Capabilities**: Pump-probe, reflectance, variable field (up to 5000+ Oe), variable temperature
- **Samples measured**: CrSBr BFA, CrSBr+BSCCO heterostructures

### Unknown cryostat (tMoTe₂ main dataset)
- **Measurements**: PL, Reflectance, RMCD across 4 cooldown runs
- **Sample**: alternating-twist trilayer MoTe₂, θ ≈ 3.9°
- **Field**: up to 8T (z-axis)
- **Temperature**: down to 1.65K

## Measurement Modalities
- **PL**: Photoluminescence (spectrally resolved)
- **Reflectance / dR/R**: Differential reflectance, circular polarization resolved
- **RMCD**: Reflective magnetic circular dichroism (hysteresis loops)
- **Pump-probe**: Time-resolved (OptiCool, CrSBr)
