**Observation**

The knowledge net distinguishes two optical roles:

Passive optical probes: trion PL and RMCD are described as filling-sensitive readouts of integer and fractional Chern states in twisted MoTe2, with reported signatures near `nu = -1, -2/3, -3/5` (`claim:signatures_of_fractional_quantum_anomalous_hall_states_in_twisted_mote2_bilayer_trion_pl_and_rmc`; `lab_assistant/knowledge/papers/xu_group_recent_moire_states.md`). Magneto-optics, combined with local compressibility, is also listed as bulk evidence for integer and fractional Chern states (`claim:thermodynamic_evidence_of_fractional_chern_insulator_in_moire_mote2_local_compressibility_plus_m`; `lab_assistant/knowledge/papers/transport_and_thermodynamics.md`).

Active optical control: a separate claim says optical pumping is now being used as a control knob for integer and fractional Chern states, not only as a probe (`claim:optical_control_of_integer_and_fractional_chern_insulators_optical_pumping_is_now_being_used_as`; `paper:optical_control_of_integer_and_fractional_chern_insulators`). A related promoted claim reports zero-field optical switching of integer and fractional Chern ferromagnets, magnetic bistate cycling, and spatially resolved domain-wall writing in twisted MoTe2 (`claim:promoted_2508_optical_switching_domain_walls`).

**Inference**

The passive-probe story is: PL/RMCD/magneto-optics reveal filling, magnetization, or bulk/edge/topological-state signatures without being framed as intentionally changing the phase. The active-control story is stronger: optical pumping is treated as a manipulation axis capable of switching or writing Chern-ferromagnetic configurations, including fractional states. Thus the local net sees optics moving from diagnostic readout toward phase and domain control.

**Uncertainty**

The supplied context does not include experimental parameters, mechanisms, pump helicity details, switching thresholds, timescales, or quantitative comparison between integer and fractional switching. Confidence is marked medium for the main optical-control and optical-probe claims, so the net supports the distinction but not a detailed mechanism-level account.
