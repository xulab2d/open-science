**Observation**

Optical probes in Xu Lab-style moire systems can be primary state-sensitive evidence, including PL, RMCD, MOKE, and pump-probe, but the context does not by itself establish that a `nu=-1/3` optical feature is an FQAH state (`claim:optical_probes_primary_evidence`).

Known stronger FQAH evidence in twisted MoTe2 includes direct Hall-plateau transport at fractional values, cited for IQAH/FQAH claims (`claim:observation_of_fractionally_quantized_anomalous_hall_effect_direct_hall_plateau_evidence_for_iqa`). Competing physics is plausible: moire-trapped interlayer excitons can produce strong valley-selective optical signatures independent of electronic topological order (`claim:signatures_of_moire_trapped_valley_excitons_in_mose2_wse2_heterobilayers_interlayer_excitons_can`). Antiferromagnetic and FCI ground states can also compete in intermediate-angle MoTe2, with electric-field switching between regimes (`paper:evidence_of_competing_ground_states_between_fractional_chern_insulator_and_antif`).

**Inference**

For a claimed optical `nu=-1/3` FQAH signature, consider at least these alternatives:

1. A real fractional Chern/FQAH state with optical contrast as the readout.
2. A non-topological valley-polarized or ferromagnetic correlated state giving MCD/RMCD contrast without fractional Hall response.
3. A competing AFM or field-tuned magnetic phase that mimics or suppresses the expected optical signal.
4. Moire exciton/trion spectral shifts, localization, oscillator-strength changes, or charging effects that track filling but are not direct evidence of fractional Hall topology.

Evidence that would separate them: direct fractional Hall quantization at the same filling; bulk incompressibility from compressibility/thermodynamic probes; optical Landau-fan/Streda behavior tied to the expected fractional Hall conductance; consistent zero-field time-reversal breaking; and systematic displacement-field, magnetic-field, and density dependence showing the feature is locked to `nu=-1/3` rather than to generic exciton/trion resonances (`claim:observation_of_fractionally_quantized_anomalous_hall_effect_direct_hall_plateau_evidence_for_iqa`; `claim:thermodynamic_evidence_of_fractional_chern_insulator_in_moire_mote2_compressibility_plus_magneto`; `claim:promoted_2304_fqah_streda_optical_mcd`).
