**Inference**

The knowledge net distinguishes two optical roles. PL/RMCD/magneto-optics are evidence channels: they detect filling-, magnetization-, or bulk-state signatures associated with Chern phases. Optical pumping is a manipulation channel: light helicity/drive is treated as a knob that can switch or write the correlated topological magnetic state.

For a PI-level framing: passive optics help identify and map integer/fractional Chern states; active optics aim to reconfigure them, including switching Chern ferromagnets and writing domain walls.
