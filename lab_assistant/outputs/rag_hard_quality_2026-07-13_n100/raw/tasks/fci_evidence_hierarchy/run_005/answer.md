5. **Compressibility alone**  
   Compressibility is powerful for detecting incompressibility, but the supplied context emphasizes it in combination with magneto-optics for bulk Chern-state evidence (`claim:thermodynamic_evidence_of_fractional_chern_insulator_in_moire_mote2_local_compressibility_plus_m`).  
   Rules out: a metallic or gapless state at the relevant filling. Alone, it does not uniquely identify Chern topology.

**Inference**

For an FCI claim, the hierarchy is: **bulk thermodynamic/compressibility evidence tied to magnetic/topological response > spatially resolved local probes > quantized/fractional transport > optical RMCD/PL fingerprints > compressibility alone**. The strongest case is convergent: incompressible bulk, fractional/topological transport, local bulk-edge consistency, and optical magnetic/filling signatures all appearing at the same filling.
