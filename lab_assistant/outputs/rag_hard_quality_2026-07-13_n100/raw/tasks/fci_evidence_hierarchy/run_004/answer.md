**Inference**

- **Transport rules out a trivial metal** if quantized or edge-dominated signatures are present, but by itself may not rule out edge-only conduction, disorder percolation, or non-bulk origins.
- **Compressibility rules out a simple compressible metallic state** by showing an incompressible bulk gap, but does not by itself establish Chern topology.
- **Local probe rules out purely global-transport artifacts** by checking whether bulk and edge features appear spatially where expected.
- **Optical/RMCD/PL rules out a completely featureless filling dependence** and supports magnetic/valley/correlated-state assignments, but is weaker as standalone topological evidence.
- **Thermodynamics combined with compressibility and magneto-optics best rules out “transport-only” interpretations**, because it ties the state to bulk incompressibility and magnetic/topological signatures.

The supplied context does not provide a single formal hierarchy or explicit exclusion table. The ranking above is an evidence-strength inference from the cited claims, especially the statement that thermodynamic/local-compressibility/magneto-optic evidence is needed beyond transport.
