**Observation**

1. **Transport**: direct anomalous/fractional Hall and edge conductance signatures are closest to the defining topological response. Example: fractional QSH transport at `nu=3` with zero anomalous Hall response and edge conductance is cited in `claim:promoted_2402_fqsh_nu3_edge_conductance`. It rules out a purely optical or magnetic anomaly, but by itself does not prove bulk incompressibility or spatial homogeneity.

2. **Thermodynamics / compressibility**: local compressibility plus magneto-optics is described as bulk evidence for integer and fractional Chern states (`claim:thermodynamic_evidence_of_fractional_chern_insulator_in_moire_mote2_local_compressibility_plus_m`, `lab_assistant/knowledge/papers/transport_and_thermodynamics.md`). It rules out a merely metallic or gapless state at the target filling, and strengthens the case that the transport feature comes from an incompressible bulk phase.

3. **Local probe**: local probes can resolve both bulk and edge structure rather than relying only on global transport (`paper:local_probe_of_bulk_and_edge_states_in_a_fractional_chern_insulator`; `claim:local_probe_of_bulk_and_edge_states_in_a_fractional_chern_insulator_local_probes_can_resolve_bot`). This rules out interpretations based only on device-averaged transport, such as edge-only artifacts, percolation, or strong spatial inhomogeneity.

4. **Optical / RMCD / PL**: trion PL and RMCD are filling-sensitive optical probes of integer and fractional Chern states in twisted MoTe2, with signatures near `nu = -1, -2/3, -3/5` (`claim:signatures_of_fractional_quantum_anomalous_hall_states_in_twisted_mote2_bilayer_trion_pl_and_rmc`). They rule out a featureless charge state and can track valley/magnetic/order changes, but are indirect evidence for fractional topology.

5. **Optical control**: optical pumping is noted as an active control knob for integer and fractional Chern states (`claim:optical_control_of_integer_and_fractional_chern_insulators_optical_pumping_is_now_being_used_as`). This supports controllability/coupling to the phase, but is not by itself a primary diagnostic.

**Inference**

A practical evidence hierarchy for claiming a fractional Chern insulator is:

**transport quantization + bulk thermodynamic incompressibility > local spatial confirmation of bulk/edge structure > compressibility or thermodynamics alone > optical/RMCD/PL correlations > optical control response alone.**

The strongest case is multi-probe: transport establishes the topological response; compressibility/thermodynamics establish a bulk gap; local probes show the response is not an averaged artifact; RMCD/PL identify correlated magnetic/valley/filling-dependent order.

**Uncertainty**

The supplied context does not give full numerical quantization values, activation gaps, entropy/magnetization data, or detailed criteria for thermodynamic measurements. It also does not specify which thermodynamic observable beyond compressibility/magneto-optics was measured, so the ranking treats “thermodynamics” mainly as bulk-gap evidence rather than a standalone proof of fractional Chern order.
