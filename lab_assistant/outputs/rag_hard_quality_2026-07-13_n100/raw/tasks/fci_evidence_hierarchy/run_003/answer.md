**Observation**

1. **Transport**: strongest direct evidence for a fractional Chern/QAHE response when it shows fractional Hall conductance with insulating longitudinal behavior; related transport evidence is noted for fractional QSH states in twisted MoTe2 (`claim:promoted_2402_fqsh_nu3_edge_conductance`). It rules out an ordinary trivial insulator or simple metal as the sole explanation, but by itself may not prove the response is a homogeneous bulk phase.

2. **Compressibility / thermodynamics**: bulk incompressibility is high-value evidence because local compressibility plus magneto-optics is described as “bulk evidence for integer and fractional Chern states” (`claim:thermodynamic_evidence_of_fractional_chern_insulator_in_moire_mote2_local_compressibility_plus_m`; `lab_assistant/knowledge/papers/transport_and_thermodynamics.md`). It rules out a merely conductive edge/channel network and supports a gapped many-body bulk state.

3. **Local probe**: local probes can resolve both bulk and edge structure rather than relying only on global transport (`paper:local_probe_of_bulk_and_edge_states_in_a_fractional_chern_insulator`; `claim:local_probe_of_bulk_and_edge_states_in_a_fractional_chern_insulator_local_probes_can_resolve_bot`). They rule out spatial inhomogeneity, edge-only artifacts, and unresolved phase separation as sufficient explanations.

4. **Optical / RMCD / PL**: trion PL and RMCD are filling-sensitive optical probes of integer and fractional Chern states in twisted MoTe2, with reported signatures near fractional fillings (`claim:signatures_of_fractional_quantum_anomalous_hall_states_in_twisted_mote2_bilayer_trion_pl_and_rmc`). They rule out a purely transport-contact artifact and track magnetism/valley polarization, but are indirect for fractional topology.

5. **Optical control / pump response**: optical pumping can act as an active control knob for integer and fractional Chern states (`claim:optical_control_of_integer_and_fractional_chern_insulators_optical_pumping_is_now_being_used_as`). It supports coupling to the order parameter, but alone does not establish a quantized topological state.

**Inference**

Best evidence hierarchy for an FCI is: **transport quantization + bulk compressibility/thermodynamic gap + local spatial confirmation** as the core case. **RMCD/PL/optical** are important supporting probes because they identify filling-dependent magneto-optical structure, but they should not outrank bulk gap and quantized response.
