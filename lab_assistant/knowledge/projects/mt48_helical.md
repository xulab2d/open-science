# MT48 Helical

Status: active project summary. Owners/collaborators inferred from root/deck names: Shuai Yuan, Weijie Li, and Christiano Wang Beach.

## Broad Overview

MT48 is a 4-degree helical 1+1+1 tMoTe2 project using PL and dR/R dual-gate optical maps to track filling-dependent and magnetic-looking structure. The project is adjacent to transport interpretation: optical features are discussed in the language of fillings and insulating/fractional regimes.

Canonical NAS root:
- `/Volumes/Xu Lab/tMoTe2_Measuring/Shuai_CWB_MT48_attodry911`

## Recent Developments

June 2026 evidence includes `Shuai_WJL_MT48_attodry911_run2.pptx`, PL dual-gate maps at 0 T and 0.1 T, and dR/R/PL maps. Deck text reports regions polarized at zero field and field-induced polarization in another region.

The deck connects optical structure to `nu = -1`, `nu = -4/3`, a large insulating state around `D = 0` and `n = -6`, and possible lower-doping fractional features. These assignments look useful but should be treated as calibration-dependent.
