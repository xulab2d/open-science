## Broad Overview

D88 Coil is the Attodry522 coil-measurement branch of the D88 AAA 1+1+1 tMoTe2 project. It focuses on MCD/RMCD/domain-style measurements and linecuts intended to distinguish ordinary magnetic-boundary contrast from possible nontrivial/topological MCD.

## Recent Developments

June 2026 evidence is strong: notebooks such as `Domain_identification.ipynb` and `Field_dependence.ipynb`, linecut `.mat` files through 2026-06-21, and the `Yue_D88_1+1+1_AAA_4_attodry522.pptx` deck.

## Watchpoints

- Modulation maps and pulsed linecuts have different resolution and broadening tradeoffs.
- Do not compare coil-MCD and standard RMCD amplitudes as equivalent without mode metadata.
- Domain identification should stay tied to the exact linecut/spatial coordinate system used in the notebook.
