# D88

Status: active project-family summary. Main owners/collaborators: Courtney Baier, Christiano Wang Beach, and Yue Sun.

## Broad Overview

D88 is now a benchmark family for AAA 1+1+1 tMoTe2 optical and magneto-optical measurements. It spans the original Attodry911 root, a run2 Attodry522 root, and Yue's Attodry522 coil-measurement branch.

The recurring observables are RMCD, PL, reflectance, optical fan diagrams, hysteresis, domain/MCD linecuts, and derivative-style energy tracking such as `dE/dn` when PL intensity dips are subtle.

## Recent Developments

The main D88 root has June 2026 high-value activity. Yue's coil branch has strong June evidence through `Domain_identification.ipynb`, `Field_dependence.ipynb`, linecut `.mat` files through 2026-06-21, and a D88 deck that identifies fab/measurement provenance.

The working story is D88 as a comparison standard for RMCD hysteresis at fillings such as `nu = -1` and `nu = -2/3`, optical fan features, and whether coil-MCD/domain linecuts separate ordinary magnetic-boundary contrast from possible nontrivial MCD.

## Watchpoints

- Modulation and pulsing coil modes are not interchangeable observables.
