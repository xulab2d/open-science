# A18 BAAB

Status: active project summary. Owners inferred from the project root: Zengde She and Weijie Li.

## Broad Overview

A18 is a BAAB/BAB/twisted-region optical project in the tMoTe2 A-series. The project uses reflectance, polarized reflectance, resonance scans, and dual-gate maps to identify region-dependent excitonic structure and possible weak polarization features.

Canonical NAS root:
- `/Volumes/Xu Lab/tMoTe2_Measuring/Zengde_Weijie_A18_BAAB_oldattodry`

## Recent Developments

June 2026 evidence includes `Zengde_Weijie_A18_BAAB_oldattodry_measure.pptx`, polarized dual-gate maps, reflectance filter-difference scripts, resonance scans, and BAAB/BAB region maps.

The working resonance picture includes weak features near about 1.118 eV, 1.08 eV, and 1.057 eV. A possible polarization feature appears on the 1.06 eV resonance, but the deck also notes asymmetry and possible AA-interface relaxation, so this is not yet a settled magnetic assignment.

## Watchpoints

- Reference choice is central; bare-hBN and dense-doping references may not give equivalent contrast.
- Region assignment among ABA bilayer, BB bilayer, BAAB, tBB, and BAB should be checked before comparing maps.
- Treat weak R-L structure as a candidate signal until repeated across spots and references.
