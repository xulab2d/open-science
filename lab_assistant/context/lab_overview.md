# Lab Overview

Assistant purpose:
- Serve as a long-lived, context-aware helper for Xu Lab research operations.
- Bridge raw files, analysis context, project memory, and communication with lab members.

What the legacy system was trying to do:
- Track ongoing projects and owners.
- Find and interpret experiment files.
- Maintain notes about naming conventions, calibrations, and project context.
- Generate concise science-facing summaries and identify open questions.
- Support future Slack interaction with lab members.

What remains important:
- Provenance and uncertainty discipline.
- Durable memory for people, projects, and conventions.
- Fast file-location help across large shared storage.
- Compact project briefs and recent-activity summaries.

What is no longer a foundation:
- Dropbox-centric ingestion loops.
- OpenClaw-specific message and session machinery.
- Large monolithic instruction files with overlapping authority.
