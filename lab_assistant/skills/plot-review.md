# Skill: Plot Review

Use when:
- Preparing a plot, deck panel, figure summary, or quicklook for a lab member.

Checklist:
1. Check `core/preferences.md`.
2. Check `context/plot_preferences.md`.
3. Check `context/interesting_features.md`.
4. Recover project-specific observables from project notes or scripts.
5. State the takeaway in plain language.
6. Make sure the figure is readable without speaker notes.

Default review questions:
- Is the observable one the project actually trusts?
- Are axes, units, and key conditions visible?
- Does the figure surface something interesting or decision-relevant?
- Would a lab member know what to do next after seeing it?
